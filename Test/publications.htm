Institute Faculty and Student Publications
This section of the Institute site has been designed to provide a forum for the publication of on-line reports and Internet Resource Collections written and or compiled by CSUMB Faculty and Students.  In addition, selected publications by the Director are listed for the purposes of providing students with information regarding the Director's current research interests and areas of publication.
