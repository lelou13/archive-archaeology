Culture/Equity
Met through required courses, SBSC 300A/B.
 
Language
Students must demonstrate via assessment, competency and literacy in a language other than English, at the ACTFL Novice-High level OR complete, with a grade of "C" or better, a Language learning experience through the the third semester or 201 level.
Service Learning
Met through the completion of any 2 unit course designated as Service Learning (SL).
Technology
Met through CST 101 or SBSC 218/318. Students who do not wish to complete CST 101 or SBSC 218/318, may be assessed for their competency in this area. If students wish to be assessed they can do so by registering for, and successfully completing, ASMT 430.
