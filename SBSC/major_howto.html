How to Fulfill Graduation Requirements

  • Prior Learning
Faculty assessment of competencies achieved through prior learning from personal or career experience.
  • Traditional Course Work
This may include competencies attained through other institutions of learning achieved ascredits but assessed by the Social and Behavioral Sciences Center (SBSC) in relationshipto the required competencies.
  • Advanced, Self-Learning Academic Program
This option allows students to pursue a self-paced, flexible, faculty-coordinated, independent learning program. It utilizes a variety of self-learning methods and competency assessment. Learners may use computer-mediated on-line instruction; computer-based learning modules; independent study; service-based learning; and internships to achieve the required competencies.
  •  CSUMB Learning Experience Modules
    •  
    This option allows students to pursue the required competencies by enrolling in the
    activities listed in CSUMB Catalog and in accord with their learning plans.
    AND
  • An Approval of an Individual Learning Plan (ILP)
The Individual Learning Plan stipulates short and long term goals regarding pathways to fulfilling all graduation requirements required by the Social and Behavioral Scineces Center and by California State University, Monterey Bay. The ILP further serves to articulate the educational and professional goals of the SBS major.
  • A Graduation Portfolio
This contains documentation of achieved competencies, including SBSC Major Learning Outcomes (MLO's).
  • Passing grades
    •  
    Students must complete all courses required by the Social and Behavioral Sciences Center and California State University Monterey Bay with passing grades.
