Online Journals
Note:The Online Journal provides a forum for student and visitor feedback, interaction, and critical thinking and writing.  Each week, those preparing "class journal" entries should respond to questions raised by both your professor and fellow students within your respective class section.  In addition, you should be prepared to comment on readings and materials covered in class and assigned for out of class contexts.  For visitors to this site, those wishing to contribute to observations on Institute projects, or ask questions about Institute activities, may do so within this venue.
