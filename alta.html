The Projects of the Institute of Archaeology

The Alta California Mission Research Project serves as the archaeological and ethnohistoric arm of the early California studies component of the CSUMB Institute of Archaeology. Currently, this project is engaged in the archaeological and historical study of the Old Mission so as to assess the historic preservation needs of the old mission and community of San Juan Bautista. Student projects and project-based learning experiences currently center on the development of resources for the historic preservation and archaeological salvage of the Old Mission of San Juan Bautista. All project efforts are geared to the excavation, documentation, and restoration of the archaeological and historical collections of the mission while provisioning a Museum Education Project program operated collaboratively by the students of the Institute of Archaeology and the mission and community of San Juan Bautista.
