Release and Waiver Forms
The release and waiver forms are required of students enrolled in any class or project-related activity that requires lab or field time.  The liability waiver is required for all students and is one way of acknowledging that project-related precautions and potential hazards have been reviewed by the instructor and student.  The multimedia release is the equivalent of a "photo release" in that it permits Dr. Ruben Mendoza and the Institute of Archaeology to use images of you, produced in lab or field contexts, for the purposes of publication or multimedia.  See details of forms for further information:

Modern Material Cultures Survey Forms
The Modern Material Cultures Project of the Institute of Archaeology provides a forum for student field projects centered on the study of urban subcultures and modern material culture more generally.  One aspect of the this project entails the Garbage Project survey of the Fort Ord facility and its community.  The attached .pdf forms are intended for students engaged in said study.  Such forms are to be used only by those students who have obtained written permission from both Professor Mendoza and project participants prior to initiating such a community study.
