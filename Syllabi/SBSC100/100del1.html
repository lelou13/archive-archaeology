SBSC 100A

Introduction to the Social and Behavioral Sciences

Major Deliverable 1

Produce a written description of an article from an approved social science journal (specifically any of the areas of anthropology, archaeology, social history, or cultural geography). Your description will consist of no more than a 500 to 750 word essay. The essay will identify and position the author within the social sciences, and serve to assess how that position influenced what he or she wrote, and will describe what the article was about while at the same time providing an overview of the major points presented. In preparing your essay, you should provide examples of the kinds of evidence the author used and indicate what types of primary and secondary sources he or she consulted. Finally, you will be required to explain how the author of your respective social science essay applied one of the theories discussed in class, and you will in turn assess the validity of the author's respective conclusions.
