SBSC Professional Assessment Lab

Professor Ruben G. Mendoza, Ph.D.
Director, Institute of Archaeology


  • The laboratory section will be run in seminar fashion with the instructor(s) facilitating the weekly discussions and presentation of materials and deliverables.
  • Students will be expected to lead and participate in discussions, according to assignments made by instructor.
  • All assignments must be turned in electronically, via email to the instructor.
  • Deliverables assignments will be posted at this site under "Lab Deliverables" and "Major Deliverables."
  • A portfolio with all required "Petition to Major" documents will be turned in at the end of the semester.  Electronic portfolio's are encouraged. All portfolios must be submitted in the required format.
  • Evaluation is based entirely on the completion of the required portfolio and related Lab Deliverables.
  • A grade of Incomplete will be assessed for portfolios not meeting the requirements of the ILP and "Petition to Major.".
