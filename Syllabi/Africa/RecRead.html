African Civilizations

Those recommended readings listed below have been selected for their supplementary value.  The Asante and Abarray (1996) text provides a wide range of narratives covering the African and African American cultural heritages.  The Martin and O'Meara (1995) volume contains cultural overviews and up to date mappings of the region.

Recommended Readings:

Asante, Molefi Kete, and Abu S. Abarry (eds.)

1996   African Intellectual Heritage.  Philadelphia: Temple University Press.

Martin, Phyllis M., and Patrick O'Meara (eds.)

1995   Africa (Third Edition).  Bloomington: Indiana University Press.

Phillipson, David W.

1985   African Archaeology.  Cambridge: Cambridge University Press.

Reader, John

1997   Africa: A Biography of the Continent.  New York: Alfred A. Knopf.

Schmidt, Peter R. (ed.)

1996   The Culture & Technology of African Iron Production.  Gainesville: University Press of Florida.

Vogel, Joseph O. (ed.)

1997   Encyclopedia of Precolonial Africa.  Walnut Creek: Altamira Press, Sage Publications, Inc..
