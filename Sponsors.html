Recent Sponsorships
The recent publication of Old Mission San Juan Bautista: An Educational Coloring Book (November, 1999) was a project made possible by the students of the CSUMB Institute of Archaeology, the Old Mission Gift Shop, the Gabilan Conservation Camp, and the Vocational Graphic Arts/Offset Print Program of the Correctional Training Facility, Soledad, California.  Special thanks to Mr. Koney Eng and Sally Mapes of the Offset Print Program, Mr. Jerry Smith of the Correctional Training Facility (Soledad), and the Rev. Edward Fitz-Henry of Old Mission San Juan Bautista.
Community Sponsors
The following community groups, organizations, institutions, and businesses have assisted and or sponsored the events and community outreach activities of the CSUMB Institute of Archaeology and Museum Education Project.  We actively seek your support so that we can continue to offer the kinds of community outreach and education programs facilitated by you and our students.  If you would like to contribute to the Museum Education Project, please contact the the California State University Monterey Bay "Foundation" and make your contribution to the Museum Education Project Fund.  If you would like further information on the activities and service projects of the CSUMB Institute of Archaeology, please contact Ms. Lilly Martinez at 831-582-4364, or Professor Ruben Mendoza at 831-582-3760 or by e-mail at ruben_mendoza@monterey.edu.
