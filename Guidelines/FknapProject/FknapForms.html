Flintknapping and Microwear Analysis Forms

The Ancient Cultures and Technology Lab of the Institute of Archaeology provides a context within which students may engage lab projects derived from the field of experimental archaeology.  In this instance, the following forms are to be used in the context of the flintknapping (or stone tool) exercises conducted in the flintknapping lab.  The codes or acronyms used in each section of the "Microwear Analysis Lab Specimen Record" (Mendoza, 1994) will be described and defined more specifically within the context of the labs themselves.  For additional definitions and descriptions, see Keeley (1980).
