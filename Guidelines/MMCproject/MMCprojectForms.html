Modern Material Cultures Survey Forms
The Modern Material Cultures Project of the Institute of Archaeology provides a forum for student field projects centered on the study of urban subcultures and modern material culture more generally.  One aspect of the this project entails the Garbage Project survey of the Fort Ord facility and its community.  The attached .pdf forms are intended for students engaged in said study.  Such forms are to be used only by those students who have obtained written permission from both Professor Mendoza and project participants prior to initiating such a community study.
