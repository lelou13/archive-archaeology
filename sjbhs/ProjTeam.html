The Virtual Museum Team
The Luck Museum web site is the result of a collaboration between the students and faculty of the Museum Education Project of the Institute of Archaeology of CSU Monterey Bay and the San Juan Bautista Historical Society.  The project team currently consists of several student and community volunteers.  Among the community volunteers currently working to assist the project team with the buildout of an Online Museum and Society Web Site are Georgiana Gularte, Carla Hendershot, and Luck Library librarian and Director Pat Larkin, and Barbara Franco.

Project team members include Theodore Richter, Margarita Gonzales, Carolina Sordia, and Vahina Boudouani.

Professor Ruben Mendoza is the project advisor and director of the Museum Education Project.  He may be reached at 831-582-3760, or by E-mail at ruben_mendoza@monterey.edu.
