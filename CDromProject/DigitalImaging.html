Digital Imaging and Editing

    The Digital Image Editor:  Cenan Pirani is a student in the Communications, Science and Technology program at CSU Monterey Bay, and has worked on a variety of funded projects within the Social and Behavioral Sciences Center at our campus.  Cenan brought his skills as a Photoshop technician to the creation of hundreds of images that were systematically edited and rendered as icons with transparent backgrounds used in both the CD-ROM project and on this website.  See the "Background" section of this portion of the website for a view of some of Cenan's handiwork.
