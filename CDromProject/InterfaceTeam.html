Interface Design and Development

   The Interface Design Technician:  Christian Graves is currently a student at Everett Alvarez High School and has been instrumental in the maintenance and upgrading of the ASTV Institute server and in the Java script development of some portions of the website.  Christian has long-term experience with the development of html, Java, C++, and related programming languages, and has worked on various projects for the Institute since the summer of 1998.  His role with the CD-ROM project has been that of Interface design technician and consultant.  Christian is here pictured working with both Flash and Photoshop software on the development of icons used in the design of the main interface of the CD-ROM.
