Screen 6: Under the eighth icon on the button bar, otherwise known as "Mission Archives," the viewer will find a number of sub-menus devoted to topics ranging from "Slide Shows" to "Mission Music."  In this instance, the mission music was recorded from the original scores used in the earliest days of the mission.  Dr. David Shaul, a renowned ethnomusicologist, linguist, and harpist prepared the scores used in this CD-ROM.
