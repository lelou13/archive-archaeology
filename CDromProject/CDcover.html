CD-ROM Cover Art
The cover design was first conceived by Charles Pina using a color photo transformed into a black and white image.  The cover name went from the longer and more ponderous "Old San Juan: The Art, Archaeology and History of an Early California Community" to "Old San Juan: A Mission Media Project."  While the original title was more descriptive, it was also a bit more awkward for use as cover art.  The back cover was designed by Christian Graves and was made to convey that basic information needed by the viewer to ascertain the publication's basic contents.
