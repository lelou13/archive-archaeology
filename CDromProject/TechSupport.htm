Photo Collections and Digital Archive Development

   The Photo Collections Manager:  Linda Mendoza is currently a student in the Liberal Studies and Teacher Education programs of CSU Monterey Bay.  Her participation in the Museum Education Project began in the Fall of 1995, and her long-term experience with the project led to her selection as the photo collections manager for the project.  Because of the literally thousands of transparency images that needed to be identified and sorted, Ms. Mendoza was instrumental in facilitating the selection of images for the CD-ROM project.  Linda is here shown sorting and editing images on a portable light table.
