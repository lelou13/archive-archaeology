Student projects undertaken with this task group or module may vary; however, four major themes serve to define the modern material cultures project area and its project-based learning modules. The four principal themes or modules include:
 
  • The Garbology Lab:

    • A field study of modern trash disposal practices from central coast localities for clues regarding human behavior, nutrition, and waste management.
     
  • The Undocumented Heritage Lab:

    • Local oral history and historic photograph collections field study.
     
  • The Urban Subcultures Lab:

    • Examination of urban subcultures and countercultures, and their respective material cultural forms, including: graffiti, murals, clothing, tattoos, vehicles, and related social forms and markers.
     
  • The Informal Economies Lab:

    • Field studies of the material cultures and economies of informal markets such as flea markets, swap meets, garage sales, craft fairs, and related community-based service industries.
