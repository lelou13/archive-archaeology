Why should I take SBSC/ProSeminar 100?

  • SBSC 100 is a unique collaborative learning opportunity
  • SBSC 100 gives you a foundation for meeting major learning outcomes (MLO's) in the SBSC, as well as in the Liberal Studies and Global Studies Majors (see CPEC Accreditation Report SBSC 1996, page 15).
  • SBSC 100 breaks new instructional ground through its innovative use of technology and peer collaboration.
