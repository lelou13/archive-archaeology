Final Portfolio Requirements

Description: The final portfolio of your work should reflect that content, quality, professionalism, and organization that you wish to have assessed as per your overall learning experience. As such, the contents of your final portfolio should include all work deemed critical to your overall learning experience.

Where "Lab Deliverables" are concerned, you need only to have signed your name -- or submitted notes or answers -- to your specific team's response to the Lab Deliverable activity for any one day. Where "Major Deliverables" are concerned, all such Major Deliverables should be included in your final portfolio for full credit.


Your Final Portfolio should include the following:

3. "Table of Contents" specific to Major Deliverables, including (a) Term Project, (b) Project Proposal, (c) Mid- and End Term Self Assessments, (d) semester's class notes, and (e) additional materials related to in-class activities or Lab Deliverables (where available).
