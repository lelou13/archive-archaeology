The Virtual Museum
The primary objective of the Virtual Museum's Projects Lab is with the development of an electronic repository of archaeological, ethnographic, ethnohistoric, and GlS-based materials -- including documents, photographs, graphics, video, VR animation, and computer generated text and database materials. Students engaged in project development with the Institute's Virtual Museum Projects Lab will facilitate the on-line and CD-ROM collection and publication of archaeological, ethnohistoric, ethnographic, and GlS-based project materials developed by the Institute of Archaeological Science, Technology and Visualization through its various lab and field projects.
