Term Learning Requirements

This learning experience will encompass both Lab Deliverables (LD's) and Major Deliverables (MD's).  Lab Deliverables center on those in class and hands-on projects conducted during lab and class time.  Major Deliverables are those that center on text-based exercises, term projects and research reports.  An example of the Lab Deliverable includes "LD1: Video Review" in which you will review and comment (in writing) on the content of an archaeology video during class time.  An example of a Major Deliverable includes "MD1: Self Assessment" in which you will comment on your progress in completing assignments up to and including the mid-point of the semester.

Term Deliverables: The total number of Lab Deliverables will vary from term to term, and will depend on progress in meeting the goals of the learning experience.  This semester, we will target a total of approximately five Lab Deliverables, and five Major Deliverables.  Please bear in mind that the Lab Deliverables are intended to chart your progress in hands-on activities and participation and attendance.  As such, I will conduct in-class or field-based Lab Deliverables as these become necessary to assessing your participation and attendance.
