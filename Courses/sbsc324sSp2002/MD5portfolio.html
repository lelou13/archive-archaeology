Major Deliverable 5:
Final Report, End Term Assessment, and Portfolio
Objective: The primary objective for the final portfolio is that of preparing and presenting your semester's learning experience in a singular professional format.  By collecting and binding your materials in an organized and logically coherent manner, you will convey the overall quality and completeness of your semester's efforts.  In addition, all materials developed in this course will be prepared for posting as an Online Portfolio, and as such, for the last three class meetings of the semester, you will bring to class any deliverables, Online Journal entries, and related maps, diagrams, tables, and the like in electronic or diskette format.  This material will be edited into html or web documents for posting to the Internet by the end of the term.

Format Note: All materials will be assembled into a white or black 1" thick binder with clear sleeve cover page insert for the inclusion of the cover page  on the face of the portfolio.  In addition, all written materials will be formatted in double-spaced, typewritten format with 1.5" margins on the left-hand and top margins of each and every page, and 1" margins on the right-hand and bottom margins of the page.  All documents will include your name, the course number, semester, date and consecutive page numbers from beginning to end.  Portfolios that fail to meet these requirements may be deemed incomplete for the final grade.

All semester or term portfolios require the inclusion of each of the following materials in the following order.

  • Cover Page (including: Title, Major Deliverable number, Student Name, E-mail address, Class Name and Number, Professor, Semester, University, Date).
  • Table of Contents (with page numbers)
  • Major Deliverable 1: Midterm Self Assessment
  • Major Deliverable 2: Project Proposal
  • Major Deliverable 3: Journal Review
  • Lab Deliverables 1-6
  • Lithics Lab Report 1 (Extra Credit)
  • Lithics Lab Report 2 (Option)
  • Final Term Paper or Project Report
  • Oral Report outline, notes, or summary
  • Typewritten Term Notes
  • End Term Self Assessment (minimum 3 pages)
  • Miscellaneous Attachments/Handouts
  • Diskette of Online Portfolio materials
*All of the above items should be brought to class in electronic format on 3.5" diskette for use in the development of your Online Portfolio.  We will use the Netscape Composer web editing and html development portion of the Netscape Navigator available on the class/lab computers.
