SBSC Major Learning Outcomes

SBSC Area 1: Theory

Primary knowledge in theories of the social and behavioral sciences as per concepts and applications in the area of museum anthropology and public archaeology.

Targeted Outcome: 25%
 

SBSC Area 2: Information Management, Data Retrieval, and Analysis

Knowledge and skills development required to acquire, interpret, evaluate, and use information; i.e. information management and data retrieval and analysis).

Targeted Outcome: 25%
 

SBSC Area 3: Specialized Competencies in the Social Sciences

Specialized competency in the area of museum anthropology and cultural representation, including artifact curation, exhibition design, and public programming).

Targeted Outcome: 25%

University Learning Requirements

Primary or Integrated Assessment

SERVICE LEARNING

Service Learning via mediated or mentored community participation and contributions). This course fully satisfies the upper division transfer requirement. Targeted Outcome: 50%

CULTURE & EQUITY

Culture content and culture theory as per study and reflection on specific area cultural tradition or traditions).
