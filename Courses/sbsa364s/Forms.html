Museum Education Project

Project Forms and Format Instructions

Liability Release and Waiver.  The following linked forms are required of students engaged in the community-based project efforts of the Museum Education Project.  Because of the potential hazards that may exist in some project circumstances, students are required to sign a Liability Release and Waiver form that serves to acknowledge that students were forewarned of any potential hazards that might exist on a project site.

Multimedia Release and Waiver.  Because Professor Mendoza documents the progress of most all project activities with film, video and digital forms of photography, students engaged in the community project or lab and field portions of any service activity are required to sign a Multimedia Release and Waiver Form.  The waiver in this instance acknowledges that you give Professor Mendoza permission to use images or photographs taken of you for project-related multimedia development in video, film, and digital formats.
    If you are uncomfortable with having your picture taken or used in the aforementioned circumstances, you are required to note this fact on the waiver form.  In addition, you will be required to inform any and all photographers, including other students who may be taking pictures of project-related efforts, of your desire not to have your photographs taken.  In addition, as a courtesy to any such photographers, you will in addition be required to remove yourself from situations which may involve photography of students at work.  Please ask your professor for project alternatives when photographs are being taken during project time.
