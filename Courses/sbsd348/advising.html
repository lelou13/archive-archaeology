Internet Contact: You may e-mail your questions and requests for advising via the FirstClass Intranet student e-mail accounts available to you, or you may stop by Dr. Mendoza's office during posted office hours (see Hours, above).  When submitting typewritten assignments, please e-mail one copy, and submit a second copy in print form so that I can provide comments and assessment suggestions.  Please note that the SBSC Peer Counselors are available in Building 17 for your assistance.

Important Notice: When submitting deliverables and other assignments via the internet, please provide a clear and complete identification label for your submissions. For example: Joan Doe, who is registered in the lower division portion of the Spring 1998 Maya class, and who is submitting Major Deliverable 2, would title her e-mail submission and attachment as follows: 248DOEsp00md2 (upper or lower case letters are acceptable). Any assignment that does not contain the title format in question will not be accepted for evaluation.

All electronic submissions should be dropped into the "Class Folder" specific to the section within which you are enrolled: i.e., SBSD 248 submissions should be dropped in the SBSD 248 Classes Folder, whereas SBSD 348 students will submit their assignments in the SBSD 348 Classes Folder.

If you require additional assistance with your writing, or proposal preparation, you may draw on the campus ASAP program for guidance and tutoring.
