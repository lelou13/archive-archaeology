Mesoamerican Art Identification

Description: Using your professor's visual overview and slide show of the art and sculpture of ancient Mesoamerica, be prepared to identify the last set of slides that will be shown this day. You will be required to identify the specific culture and period with which each is associated. The first nine slides are from the Dumbarton Oaks Collection in Washington, DC, while Section 2 consists of images of sites and artifacts from Dr. Mendoza's photographic studies of Mesoamerican sites from throughout Mexico and Central America.
