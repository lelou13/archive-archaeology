Major Deliverable 4

End Term Self Assessment

Description: Please prepare an End Term Self Assessment of your overall learning experience for this course. The Self Assessment Statement should consist of a minimum of two typewritten pages of text that serve to document your learning, including how well you have kept up with text and or initiated independent research related to the Maya. Your End Term Self Assessment should be included in your class portfolio along with your final term project and class notes.

Your Final Self Assessment should include the following:

 


1. General statement about your learning experience to date.
 

2. Specific statement regarding which of the Lab and Major Deliverables most interested you, and which Lab and Major Deliverables least interested you.
 

3. General statement regarding the extent to which you feel that you met your original learning objectives.
 

4. Specific statement about the research study that you undertook and the extent to which you feel that said topic lent itself to your overall learning objectives.
 

5. Specific statement regarding the extent to which the required readings assisted you in developing an understanding of Maya culture and the broader class theme.
 

6. General statement regarding the extent to which you conducted independent studies about the Maya beyond assigned class readings.
 

7. Specific statement regarding the extent to which you feel you developed a broader and more profound understanding of Maya culture.
 

8. Specific recommendations about course content and teaching strategy, and suggested revisions, that you would like to see included in future offerings of this course. For example: Any suggestions for future Lab Deliverables.
 

9. A specific statement about what you feel you could have done to further improve your performance during the course of this past semester.
 

10. A statement about how this course contributed to your overall learning experience.
