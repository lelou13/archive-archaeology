Lab Deliverable 4

Interpreting Maya Monuments

Description: Drawing on information and illustrations provided in the attached handout, your group's specific mission is to interpret the meaning of the symbolism, and known text, of the monument or monuments to which you have been assigned. Because this is a group exercise, each member of the group will need to actively participate in order to receive credit for this Lab Deliverable. The steps for interpreting the monument are provided below (please refer to your handout for additional content):

1. After a brief review of the topic by your professor, please preview the symbols included in the two handouts provided. You will find materials drawn directly from the Schele and Freidel text A Forest of Kings. The glossary and the individual pages of icons will prove useful in your interpretation of the stele or Maya monuments provided on separate pages.
 
 

2. You have three Maya monuments to choose from -- these are included on the separate monuments handout. As a task group, please select the first monument to be interpreted, and working in a coordinated fashion, select and identify individual elements on the selected monument based on the two iconography handouts provided.
 
 

3. Please take care to identify images and icons pertaining to astronomy, cosmology, and Maya beliefs regarding dynastic succession and kingship. Though not complete, especially given the large body of icons and images related to Maya beliefs, the handouts will provide a starting point for this exercise. Identify those icons specific to the following:
  · Astronomy · Cosmology · Dynastic succession · Kingship · Underworld 4. Having identified specific elements of the constellation, please be prepared to provide a brief written description of what you believe the monument or monuments are attempting to convey in the way of information. If you were to advance an interpretation of the monuments overall meaning and significance, what would that interpretation be? Please specify.
