California missions involved colonization

Colonization often included violence toward native populations

Indians received benefits of protection and meals, pots and pants, upward mobility

Many communities were subjected to this process against their will

Leading to forced acculturation-religion, education, etc.

In Texas and Arizona, the Spanish colonists brutally responded to Apache raids

Spanish would often place heads outside their presidio complexes that were there to "secure the frontier"

Protect against incursions by the British and the French, led to establishments of military provinces (Monterey was area 5)
