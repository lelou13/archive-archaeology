Major Deliverable 3: Field Journal

Author: Pedro Gonzalez.- Subject: Mission San Juan Bautista
       This past Friday (11/12/99) I went to the San Juan Mission.  I was partnered up with Melissa Fanning and we started to
       dig through the first level.  Melissa and  I found lots of stuff this Friday.  We found lots of burnt tile, some old red wood,
       colonial nails, charcoal, and some stucko.  We were able to find some blue pigmentation material that might have been used
       to paint part of the walls blue, of which we found some evidence of this.  We also found a piece of metal which seemed to have
       been used as the handle of a knife that was used in the old days because of the way the metal was wasted.  A Coors Light Beer
       can as well which was very interesting.

Author: Pedro Gonzalez.- Subject: Mission San Juan Bautista
      Date: 12/8/99.- Time: 12:00:37 PM.- Entry Num: 165
      I went to the mission this past Friday and continued to do the digging for our little space. There was some interesting stuff
      that Melissa and I found, such as some black piece that Chris said was a piece of bone. We also found a lot of red tile as
      we got deeper into the meter by meter square. It was interesting to have given those little introductions to the kids that
      came from the San Juan School. They seemed very anxious to learn what it was that we were doing and aslo to get into
      the prysical part which was the digging. Melissa and I showed them how to do the screening and told them what it was
      that we were looking for so that they would participate in helping us. All these kids were very excited to help and picked
      out anything that had red on it and any large object that seemed abnormal to them. Later that day we continued to do out
      digging and found some colonial nails and some ceramics. We are almost down to our third level. This coming Friday the
      10th we will be able to start out third level.

Author: Pedro F. Gonzalez.- Subject: Mission San Juan Bautista

      Date: 12/15/99.- Time: 2:31:34 PM.- Entry Num: 169
      This was the last day that completed the six times we were supposed to be out there. I started to dig on both my side and
      Melissas. When I started digging so that I would be able to get down to our 3rd level I started to find a lot of bugs that
      were all over the place. After a little more digging I found myself with some ceramics and a lot of red floor tile. This day
      was interesting because at the spot were I was digging I began to find the other side of the fire pitt. Inside of it was a lot of
      rock and other artifacts. Within those artifacts I found a piece of red glossy tile that looked like the pots made in Mexico
      called Ollas de Barro. I also found bone even though I am not real sure what type of bone it was or if it were from an
      animal or human a human. This was an interesting way to end my days at San Juan.
