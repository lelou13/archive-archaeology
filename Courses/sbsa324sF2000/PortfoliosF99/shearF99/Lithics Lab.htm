I had a hard time figuring out my chart. I eventually figured it out and think that they came out very well. Once I figured it out the rest of the charting was very easy.

From interpreting my chart for F-10 it seems that the Unit Square was very dense with pieces of flakes. There were many small pieces and only a few big pieces. When I look at my chart for the unit D-4 it seems to be the same thing. There are many pieces in the square that weigh very little.

If I wanted to recreate my two units I would probably use density as my factor and not weight because there are more pieces in each square. Whereas if you used weight you could put one big piece in and that wouldn’t be right. Doing this lab helped me understand how you would recreate a site with information. I
