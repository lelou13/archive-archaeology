Lab Deliverable 6: Stone Tool Analysis

Lithics Lab:

Working at the Lithics Lab was very exciting. From watching the video on flintnapping, you don't really get the real idea of how difficult it is to actually flinktnap with your own two hands and to make stone tools. AT the lab the students got a real idea of how it used to be when man had to create and make their own tools with whatever they had. Today everything is simply made for everyone through computers and machines, it's seldom that we now make objects, especially tools, with our own two hands. Making the stone tools got somewhat frustrating too, it gets difficult to try to make something specific with a stone. I know in the lab we were simply experimenting and learning, but I know the Indians depended on this and was a key part to their survival. The stone tools served as their tools for food, protection and building their homes. I didn't realize what real skill it takes to make stone tools.

Theresa F. Mendoza
SBSA 224s section 1
