Lab Deliverable 4: Excavation Methods Review

Definitions:

Site Grid- mapping an area and creating N, NS, EW grid area (method area)

Excavation tools- Tools used during the excavation, such as picks, line level (bubble level, measures depth), metric tapes, transit beam, computers, cameras, shovels are just a few essential tools in an excavation.

Stratigraphy- layers in excavation unit, layers of earth.

Datums- is main anchoring point in the site, an area where you can drive a point.

Trenches- excavating a narrower area in a site important to finding bigger sites to get artifacts or stratigraphy, gives information quickly.
