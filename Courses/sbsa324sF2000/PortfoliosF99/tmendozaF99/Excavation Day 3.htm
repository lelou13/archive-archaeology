Excavation Day 3

On my third day at the Mission, December 03, 1999, I worked on the same area I worked the last times. The area was N10W38NE1/4. It was incredibly easier this time to dig through the area because the rain had soaked through the dirt and had allowed it to soften up a lot. This area did contain a lot of floor tile, totaling up to about 1,000 grams of floor tile.  What was also strange about the site was we also found a bolt, which may be an indicator that the site was disturbed.  Other fragments that was found on the site were 50.8 g of bone, 4.5 g of glass, and 0.8 g of opalized glass. My partner and I were digging towards the end of level 2, about just 5 cm short until we would reach 40 centimeters, the objective. I was astonished at how much floor tile was found on this specific area, perhaps this was a location of floor/foundation, because the sight next to it, also contained some evidence of a wall/floor. The site was extrememly easy to dig, except the random worm colonies I would run into.
