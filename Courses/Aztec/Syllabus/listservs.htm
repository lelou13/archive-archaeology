Newsgroups & Listservers

Special interest Newsgroups, Listservers, and Blogs devoted to specialized archaeology and world prehistory topics exist all over the Internet. The list following provides a sampling of some of those sites that currently exist, or did so, when the ArchNet folks compiled a variation of the attached list in the year 2000.

I encourage each of you in the SBS 225/325 (Art of the Aztec Empire) class to register for one or more listservs devoted to Aztec or Mesoamerican archaeology, anthropology, ethnohistory, linguistics, and related studies. Such sites provide a wealth of information, and answers to some of your most pressing questions.

Of those listservs identified on this page, the most useful to communications and information pertaining to the Mexika Aztec are those of Nahuatl-L and Aztlan-L. To subscribe, simply go to the related website or email the list editor so noted below.
