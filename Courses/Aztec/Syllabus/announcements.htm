Mel Gibson.

Post of August 29, 2005: Mel Gibson is currently filming and producing yet another major motion picture, and like his "Passion of the Christ," this production makes use of an ancient language...mainly, Mayan! In this instance, the movie, which began filming in Mexico in October 2005, is titled "Apocalypto" (New Beginnings, or, the Unveiling), and represents the emergence of Maya civilization at circa 1,000 BC. It should be noted that Maya-speaking non-actors constitute the cast for the motion picture. For those interested in learning more about "Apocalypto", please see the following links:
