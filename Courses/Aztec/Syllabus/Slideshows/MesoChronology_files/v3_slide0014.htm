Variations on Chronology
Ü Formative: Identifies the early development of
civilization with Olmec and contemporaries.
Ü Protoclassic: Used to account for early and advanced or
precocious  developments (Maya).
Ü Middle Classic: Pasztory’s “Horizon” of Teotihuacan
influence.
Ü Epiclassic: Similar to Terminal Classic; Equated with
period of internecine warfare and urban collapse.
