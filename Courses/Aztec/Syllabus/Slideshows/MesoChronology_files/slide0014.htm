Variations on Chronology
ÜFormative: Identifies the early development of civilization with Olmec and contemporaries. ÜProtoclassic: Used to account for early and advanced or precocious  developments (Maya). ÜMiddle Classic: Pasztory’s “Horizon” of Teotihuacan influence. ÜEpiclassic: Similar to Terminal Classic; Equated with period of internecine warfare and urban collapse.
