Chronological Periods
Ü Paleoindian (9500-7000 BCE): Earliest Native
American settlements; Big Game Hunters.
Ü Archaic (7000-2500 BCE): Agricultural origins
and early villages.
Ü Early Preclassic (2500-900 BCE): Pox-Purron
and Ocos ceramic traditions; Olmec origins.
Ü Middle Preclassic (900-300 BCE): Height of
Olmec civilization and influence in Mesoamerica.
