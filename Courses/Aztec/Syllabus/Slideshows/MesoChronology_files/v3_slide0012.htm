Chronology and Its Basis
Ü Chronology: Based on both vertical (temporal) and
horizontal (regional) considerations.
Ü Maya Classic: Period from A.D. 300-900 was pivotal to
defining “Pre-, Classic, Post-” periods.
Ü Regional Variants: Formative, Protoclassic, Middle
Classic, Epiclassic…
Ü Secular Chronology: BCE = Before Common Era, CE =
Common Era (compare BC & AD); or, C14: BP = Before
Present (I.e., Before A.D. 1950), Radiocarbon, Maya
glyphs, thermoluminescence, obsidian hydration…
