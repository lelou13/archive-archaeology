Classic Era Developments
Ü Early Classic (250/300-600 CE): Establishment
of Long Count calendar in Maya lowlands.
Ü Middle Classic (400-700 CE): Gulf Coast and
Teotihuacan influenced/dominated horizon.
Ü Late Classic (600-900 CE): War and urban
collapse in Maya region and Mesoamerica.
Ü Early Postclassic: Rise of Toltec, Itza, Putun,
and other war-like mercantile empires.
