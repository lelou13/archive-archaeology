Sample Projects

Here you will find samples of SBS 225/325, and other Institute for Archaeology, projects and Lab and Major Deliverables completed by former students willing to share their work with you on this portion of the course and lab website. Of course, these samples are posted so as to provide you with some sense of what is expected in the way of format and content considerations for course deliverables. While I would encourage you to present your own work and research in a format that roughly resembles that presented in the samples posted below, I do nevertheless expect any and all content to reflect your own diligent efforts to collect, research, and study course, lab, and library resource content.

Note: The samples posted below have been converted to Acrobat Portable Document File format and will require that your computer has the Acrobat Reader installed. If not, please see the Text-Only portion of this website for a link to the Adobe Acrobat Reader download site.
