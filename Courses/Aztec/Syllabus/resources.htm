This course will make use of all available tools for seeing through course discussions, lab and major deliverables, and group and team efforts. While this portion of the course website will include many useful Internet links and related documents, please remember that much of what we will accomplish in this course is dependent on your timely and ongoing completion of any and all reading assignments and literature reviews deemed necessary to seeing through your completion of course requirements. Please be prepared to make use of the wide variety of search tools and electronic resources available through the CSU Monterey Bay Library resources page.
