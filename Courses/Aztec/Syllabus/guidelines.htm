Release and Waiver Forms
The release and waiver forms are required of students enrolled in any class or project-related activity that requires lab or field time. The liability waiver is required for all students and is one way of acknowledging that project-related precautions and potential hazards have been reviewed by the instructor and student. The multimedia release is the equivalent of a "photo release" in that it permits Dr. Ruben Mendoza and the Institute of Archaeology to use images of you, produced in lab or field contexts, for the purposes of publication or multimedia.
