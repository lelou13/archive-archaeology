Classic Era Developments
ÜEarly Classic (250/300-600 CE): Establishment of Long Count calendar in Maya lowlands.
ÜMiddle Classic (400-700 CE): Gulf Coast and Teotihuacan influenced/dominated horizon.
ÜLate Classic (600-900 CE): War and urban collapse in Maya region and Mesoamerica.
ÜEarly Postclassic: Rise of Toltec, Itza, Putun, and other war-like mercantile empires.
