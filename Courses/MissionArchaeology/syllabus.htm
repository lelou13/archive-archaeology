Syllabus


This portion of the SBS 260s/360s online syllabus provides an overview of lab and field program content, requirements, and project schedules for the current term. Please note that, to one extent or the other, student interest, the vagueries of weather and other field considerations, and the professor’s desire to remain true to enhancing the learning experience in a flexible and open-ended fashion, necessarily dictate that from time to time changes and or revisions to the course schedule and content may occur. Prior to any substantive changes that might be necessitated as per course content, the professor will first discuss proposed changes where needed. If you have any questions or concerns regarding program content, please ask questions and or contact your professor after class or during office hours.
