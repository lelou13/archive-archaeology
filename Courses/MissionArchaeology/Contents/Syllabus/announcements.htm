Announcements

Please frequent this portion of the website for announcements or news pertaining to class content or schedule changes and the like. Your professor will post those announcements and messages on this page and via email from archaeology_institute@csumb.edu. The most recent announcements or postings will appear as dated entries at the top of the Announcements section or column indicated at the bottom of this page. When emailing Professor Mendoza, please use the Institute for Archaeology email address at archaeology_institute@csumb.edu. All email should be appropriately titled in the subject line so that priority mail is dealt with in a timely manner.

Release and Waiver Forms. If you participate in a lab or field option that requires out of classroom activities, you will be required to sign a Liability Release and Waiver form. Said form merely acknowledges in advance that you were informed of any potential hazards, and that you willingly participated in a chosen activity despite said hazards. The Liability form will be accompanied by an in-class or field orientation to the fact that some lab projects may entail risks and or require that certain prescribed precautions be taken by any and all participants. Whereas the Liability form only applies for lab or field options that may take you away from the classroom, and or off campus, the second form is required of all students who participate in in-class or field based labs. The Multimedia Release will permit your professor to document your class labs, field research, and lab experiences by way of photography or video and related multimedia. This latter form will permit your professor to make use of photographs or videography of you in future projects or publications.
If at any time during the semester you are concerned about lab or field project options that may entail necessary lab or field precautions, or require you to sort samples that you are concerned about handling despite being provided with safety gear, please do let your professor know so that he can assign you to other project activities where applicable.
