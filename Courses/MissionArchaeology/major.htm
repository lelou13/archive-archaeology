Major Deliverables


Major Deliverables are those major project reports, midterm and end term reviews, and class presentations completed through the course of the semester. Major Deliverables in this context include Reading Reviews and related written works. Example: Both the name of the email and the attached document for Major Deliverable 1 (MD1) for the Spring 2005 offering of SBS 332 submitted by John Doe should be titled in the following manner: sbs360sSp05MD1Doe. Where sbs360s designates the class number, Sp05 the Spring 2005 term, MD1 Major Deliverable 1, and Doe the last name of the student submitting the deliverable in question.
