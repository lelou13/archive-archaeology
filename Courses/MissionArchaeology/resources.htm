This course will make use of all available tools for seeing through project lab and field investigations, lab and major deliverables, and individual and group efforts. While this portion of the course website will include many useful Internet links and related documents, please remember that much of what we will accomplish in this course is largely dependent on your active and consistent participation and timely and ongoing completion of any and all reading assignments and lab deliverables deemed necessary to satisfactory completion of any and all course requirements. Please make use of the wide variety of search tools and electronic resources available through the CSU Monterey Bay Library for project bibliographies.
