Lab & Major Deliverables
Instructions:  Lab Deliverables are those deliverables or in-class labs and activities that provide a group context for exploring the many facets of culture, society, and history pertinent to the cultures under study.  Such labs serve to document your participation in class-related activities and documentation for said labs is posted to the Online Journal located at:
http://villa.monterey.edu/courses/sbsc220/journal.htm

Major Deliverables are those major project reports, midterm and end term reviews, and class presentations completed through the course of the semester.  Major Deliverables in this context include Reading Reviews and related written works.  There will be a total of ten Lab Deliverables requiring postings to the Online Journal and five Major Deliverables for the semester and they are as follows:

LD 1: The Naming Ceremony

LD 2:  Native American and Early California Internet Sites

LD 3:  Online Library Search

Old Monterey: A Glimpse of the Past [videorecording].  By Emiko Omori (Director) and Norman Kurtin (Producer) of Design Media.  Sacramento: California Department of Parks and Recreation, 1993.

Chinese Gold: The Chinese of the Monterey Bay [videorecording].  Gold Mountain Productions. Derry, N.H.: Chip Taylor Communications, 1992.  CSU Monterey Bay Library, VC 0188 pt. 1
