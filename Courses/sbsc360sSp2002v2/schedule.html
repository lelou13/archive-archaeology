You should take note of the fact that the semester schedule is subject to change at any time.  The following schedule is intended to give you a preliminary indication of due dates for required readings, assessments, and project due dates.  Scheduled archaeology field days are also noted.  On those days or weeks where your professor has scheduled "Online Time" or "Projects Time," you may meet as a group or groups within the regularly scheduled classroom, however, you should note that no regular class meeting will take place on those days so designated.  Online Time will need to be documented by way of the Discussion Forum located at: phpbb2

Note: During scheduled "Online Time" your professor will post a [Poll] Question and create a Discussion Forum entry. You should enter the Poll question/Discussion Forum Entry and "Post Reply" within said folder.

Please note that you are required to post comments, observations, responses, and questions to the Wireless Journal on a weekly basis. Discussion Forum entries should be entered as required in providing evidence of your successful completion of Lab and Field Deliverables and related exercises.  While much of this will take place on a weekly basis during lab or field time, you should prepare to post additional weekly comments to the Discussion Forum if you are participating in the Friday excavation day at either Carmel Mission or Old Mission San Juan Bautista.
