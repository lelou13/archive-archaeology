Proto-Classic Developments
¬Cuicuilco’s Demise: Eruption of Xitle and the Rise of Teotihuacan, ca. 200 BCE.
¬Teotihuacan (Place Where Men Become Gods): 100 BCE=settlement; 100 CE= Construction of Sun Pyramid; 250 CE = Temple of Quetzalcoatl & Cuidadela; Avenue of the Dead.
¬Epiclassic Collapse: 550-650 CE=Competing mercantile interests; Teotihuacan Corridor closes; 650 CE= City Burned; 750 CE=City abandoned; 850 CE= Toltecs occupy ruined city.
¬Olmecoid Influence: Gulf Coast presence in city.
