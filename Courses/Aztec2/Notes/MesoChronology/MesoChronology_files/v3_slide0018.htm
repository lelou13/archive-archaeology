Chronology Methods
Ü Ethnohistoric Resources (Prime): Contact period
chronicles and codices used for interpretation.
Ü Archaeological Research: (Prime) Excavation and study
of archaeological sites and artifacts.
Ü Absolute vs Relative Dating: Absolute = Radiocarbon
(C14), Thermoluminescence, Dendrochronology,
Obsidian Hydration; Relative = Ceramics, Art Styles,
Architecture, etc….Maya glyphs occupy a middle
ground in this scheme…though primarily absolute.
