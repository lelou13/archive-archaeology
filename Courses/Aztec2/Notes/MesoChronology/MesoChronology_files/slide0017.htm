The Postclassic Period
ÜLate Postclassic (1250-1520 CE): Period dominated by Mixtec and Aztec civilizations.
ÜSpanish Colonial (1520-1821 CE): Conquest of Aztec and Spanish exploration, colonization, and settlement.
ÜEarly Mexican Republic (1821-1910 CE): Period extending from the fall of the Spanish empire and ending with the onset of the Mexican Revolution.
ÜMexican Revolution (1910-1920 CE): War that led to the liberal Constitution of 1917.
