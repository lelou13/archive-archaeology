Early Humans & Their Art
vMesoamerica: Human settlement 30,000 years.
vEarly Art: Tequixquiac llama pelvis carved into “coyote” – circa 10,000 years.
vPetroglyphs & Pictographs: Presence throughout Mesoamerica for most all time periods.
vEarly ceramics: 2200 BCE. Pacific coastal Guatemala (S. American influence) & Pre-Olmec Mexican Gulf Coast.
vOlmec Civilization (1500-300 BCE): Ceramics, sculpture (Monuments & figurines), mosaics, architecture, jade lapidary, exotic mirrors, etc. (e.g. El Baul, Guatemala)
