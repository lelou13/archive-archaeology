Reader Response papers are not to exceed one typewritten single-spaced page per Unit or Chapter assignment.

Please consult the online version of this schedule on an ongoing basis in order to get updates on changes that may accrue. From time to time, your professor will make revisions on the evening before, or the morning of, class meetings so as to make revisions necessitated by student participation and course progress. Note: The highlighted LD links to the left hand portion of the Lab Deliverable title assigned each week provide a direct link to the Discussion Forums located at http://archaeology.csumb.edu/phpbb2/. The Lab Deliverable title, where highlighted, constitute a direct link to the PDF or Acrobat version of the Lab Deliverable assignment or instructions for the week.

The hard copy and electronic submission of all class deliverables completed to date will be required at both midterm and end term, and should be delivered to your professor via electronic 3.5” diskette or CD copy. Otherwise, all deliverables must be submitted in hardcopy portfolio format for assessment by the specified deadlines noted in the schedule below.

See http://archaeology.csumb.edu/Guidelines/AssessmentGuides/PortfolioAssessmentFormFnl.pdf for a sample of (final) Portfolio Assessment Criteria.
