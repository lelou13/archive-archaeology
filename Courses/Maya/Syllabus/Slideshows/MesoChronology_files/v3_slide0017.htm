The Postclassic Period
Ü Late Postclassic (1250-1520 CE): Period dominated by
Mixtec and Aztec civilizations.
Ü Spanish Colonial (1520-1821 CE): Conquest of Aztec
and Spanish exploration, colonization, and settlement.
Ü Early Mexican Republic (1821-1910 CE): Period
extending from the fall of the Spanish empire and
ending with the onset of the Mexican Revolution.
Ü Mexican Revolution (1910-1920 CE): War that led to the
liberal Constitution of 1917.
