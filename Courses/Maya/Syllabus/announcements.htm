After: http://www.survivorfever.net/index11.html

Post of August 24, 2005: Just a friendly note for fans of the CBS television show "Survivor." This year's "Survivor" will take place on the site of an ancient Maya ruin in the jungles of Guatemala. As such, for those interested in what one has the option of eating or drinking in the jungle ruins of the ancient Mesoamerica, stay tuned for "Survivor" 2005. See http://www.survivorfever.net/index11.html for one take on "Survivor: Guatemala." As a start, you might compete with one another to find the Maya site of Yaxhá, Guatemala.

Mel Gibson.

Post of August 29, 2005: Mel Gibson is set to film yet another major motion picture, and like his "Passion of the Christ," this production will make use of an ancient language...mainly, Mayan! In this instance, the movie, which will begin filming in Mexico in October 2005, is titled "Apocalypto" (New Beginnings, or, the Unveiling), and is set to represent the emergence of Maya civilization at circa 1,000 BC. It should be noted that Maya-speaking non-actors will constitute the cast for the motion picture. For those interested in learning more about "Apocalypto", please see the following links:
