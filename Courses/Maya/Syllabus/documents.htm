The Documents portion of this website is intended to serve as an online venue for the posting of class-related documents, faculty publications, archaeology shareware links, and related materials relevant to course and research project content. In this section, your Professor will post links to photographs taken by him or student project participants wishing to share images with fellow students enrolled in the SBS 248/348 or related Institute courses. Such images may be used for course-related website development pertaining to the Student Projects Page. In each instance in which such images are used, the student is responsible for providing a credit line to the copyright holder. In those instances in which you may use photographs prepared by Professor Mendoza, the credit line should read as follows:

Copyright © Ruben G. Mendoza, 2007

In those instances where you may use images produced by fellow students, the format should similarly follow the format noted below:

Copyright © Full Name, Year
