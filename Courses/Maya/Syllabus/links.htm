Links

This resources section is intended to provide a venue for posting class related Internet links and resources pertinent to Maya studies. The first set of links are drawn from one of the more thorough sources for archaeology in North America…mainly, ArchNet. Other links will be posted as needed to address course content and related items of interest.

The remaining links on this page were posted so as to supplement lab content and discussion forum instructional materials. The Maya Glyph Workshops, which entail the interpretation and "transcription" of available glyph panels, will be particularly dependent on those hyperlinked instructional materials available at such sites as that of the Mayan Epigraphic Database Project.
