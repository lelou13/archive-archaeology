Syllabus

This portion of the SBS 248/348 online syllabus provides an overview of course content, in-class lab requirements, and schedules the current term. Please note that, to one extent or the other, student interest, and the professor’s desire to remain true to enhancing the learning experience in a flexible and open-ended fashion, necessarily dictates that from time to time changes and or revisions to the course schedule and content may occur. Prior to any substantive changes that might be necessitated as per course content, the professor will first discuss proposed changes where needed prior to making such changes. If you have any questions or concerns regarding any of that content noted, please feel free to ask questions and or contact your professor after class or during office hours.

Yucatán: Benjamin Perez, a Mexican linguist...reported in an article years ago...that the local name for one of the Chontal dialects was "yuka-t'an", "yuka" being an ethnic name for that group of Chontals. So the canoe traders were telling Cortez "We speak Chontal--Yuka-t'an." Courtesy: Nicholas Hopkins, 2005.
