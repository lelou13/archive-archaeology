Primary Readings

Primary readings or texts chosen for this course have been selected for the purposes of serving as guides to in-class lab exercises and discussion forum activities. The Simon and Grube (2000), and Tedlock (1996), books constitute the core readings. We will in effect begin the term with selections from the Coe and Van Stone (2001), and Tedlock (1996) texts as these provide a culture history overview of the Maya and Mesoamerica. The Simon and Grube (2000) book will in turn provide a point of departure for exploring the site by site dynastic histories of the Maya world. The Popol Vuh (Tedlock 1996) will in turn serve as an essential reference for understanding Maya world view and belief. The Coe and Van Stone (2001) text will frame the study of the Maya glyph system and its interpretation. Unlike previous semesters, we will begin the term with an introduction to Maya epigraphy and interpretation.
