You should take note of the fact that the semester schedule is subject to change at any time.  The following schedule is intended to give you a preliminary indication of due dates for required readings, assessments, and project due dates.  Scheduled archaeology field days are also noted.  On those days or weeks where your professor has scheduled "Online Time" or "Projects Time," you may meet as a group or groups within the regularly scheduled classroom, however, you should note that no regular class meeting will take place on those days so designated.  Online Time will need to be documented as that time during which you went online to communicate and collaborate with fellow students on projects, or used that time to communicate with your professor via e-mail
Please note that you are required to post comments, observations, responses, and questions into the Online Journal on a weekly basis.  While much of this will take place on a weekly basis during class time, you should prepare to post additional weekly comments in the Online Journal if you are participating in the Friday excavation day at Old Mission San Juan Bautista.
