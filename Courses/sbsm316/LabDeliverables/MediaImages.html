    Media Image Review

    Instructions:  Select at least five images from popular American magazines and or archaeology and social science journals.  The images in this instance should provide what you believe to be a realistic or accurate representation of "American" culture and society.  In addition, select at least two images from the same sources that you believe represents one other ethnic, racial, social, religious, or gender "diverse" group from within American society.  Bring these images to class and be prepared to discuss the reasons underlying your selection of the images in question.  Consider what criteria -- visual imagery and social content -- you used to select the images that you felt most accurately or appropriately portrayed American culture on the one hand, and one other "diverse" American group on the other.
     
    • Select a total of seven images from magazines or journals
    • Bring images or magazines and journals to class
    • Subdivide these images by those that represent (a) American culture, and (b) subculture.
    • Be prepared to characterize in the online journal those criteria used in your selection
