Water and Life

"Water and Life" is a photo essay which is intended to capture the varying moods that occur when water meets land. Each setting is in a slightly different location. The photographs capture the energy of water and its effects on the surrounding environment as well as its inhabitants. "Water and Life" is a collection of landscapes and portraits taken in Carmel, Big Sur, and Pebble Beach. Enjoy.

Michael Dilks
