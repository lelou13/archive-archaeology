~ Laurel Hunter ~

"(CSUMB is) a very small school and because of that we have a
different kind of community than other schools"

 

 

 

A junior studying Human Communications Laurel has ambitions of going into mediation or diplomatic relations. ~ Originally from Humboldt county, Laurel was attracted to CSUMB for the languages. ~ Laurel believes CSUMB has a different kind of community then most schools which included a wide variety of interests, backgrounds and cultures represented on campus. ~ Laurel is currently involved in Amnesty International, peer mentoring, Christmas in April, and she also serves as the HCOM faculty/student representative for her cohort. ~ Laurel got involved in these particular projects or activities because they sparked her interest in one way or another and she thought they would be interesting and that she could learn something unique from them.
