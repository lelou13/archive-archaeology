Female Leaders in the CSUMB Community:
Theme Based Photo Essay

California State University Monterey Bay established in 1994 only
occupies a small area of what used to be the Fort Ord army base.
CSUMB however is the little school that has a lot of spirit.

CSUMB students, faculty, and staff support over 38 clubs and organizations,
which is an incredible number compared to the overall small student body.
These clubs and organizations include Student Voice, Residence Hall Association,
Roteract, Rugby, the Wet and Soggies, and the list goes on. With a three to one ration
it is only be expected that a number of these clubs and organization are lead by women.
