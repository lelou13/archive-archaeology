Major Deliverable 4- Photo Essay

Introduction

For my Photo Essay I decided to take a photo trip through Fisherman's Wharf. Fisherman's Wharf has been in existence in Monterey, California since 1846. It was originally built as a pier which gave way to the, then booming, commercial fishing industry in the early 1900's. Today the wharf is a well known tourist spot that attracts visitors with it's appetizing restaurants and interesting curio shops. One can find seafood, clothing, posters, fish and chips, awesome views, and so many other interesting items and sites at the Wharf.
As you peruse my photographs, you will see that I have shown the various sides that exist among the wharf. There is the tourism industry, fishing industry, restaurant industry, educational expeditions, and the animals and plants that live on or around the wharf. The existence of the Wharf, however, relies heavily upon the abundance of tourists and locals that visit the Wharf every year. Even as a local resident who has been to the wharf multiple times, I still enjoy visiting the Wharf time and again to see what's new, or even just to visit a shop or restaurant that I have been to before.
Hopefully, within the composition of my photographs you will be able to get a feeling for what the Fisherman's Wharf is all about and how it is an interesting and colorful representation (though it is really only a small portion of the bigger "picture") of Monterey's history. Each of the pictures were chosen for their ability to convey an idea of the Wharf to those who haven't seen it yet or were perhaps curious about our history and local culture. While at the wharf try some seafood at Dockside's, have some salt water taffy at Carousel's Candy Shop, try a lobster dinner at Fisherman's Grotto, or perhaps have some Fish and Chips at Captain's Gig...there are so many different things to try and many interesting places to shop, you might find that you have to come back again! Enjoy the beautiful sights and the get a taste for Monterey.....
