Top Gun. Before you reach the end of the wharf, off to the left you will see two large boats...these boats are the whale watching boats that you can go on to see the whale migrations through our area. As you can see, the boat above is called Top Gun. The photograph was originally produced with late afternoon sun and a 35 mm camera using Provia slide film, shutter speed 125, F16.
