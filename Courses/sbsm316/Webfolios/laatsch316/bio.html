Brian's Bio

My name is Brian Laatsch and I am a student at California State University Monterey Bay. I am originally from Cupertino, California which is nestled in the western foothills of the southern San Francisco Bay Area. I attended Monta Vista High School where I played football and baseball. I enjoy many outdoor activities such as backpacking, mountain biking and snowboarding. Here at CSUMB I have continued my education and have found the resources of Monterey to be very helpful. The high-tech atmosphere on campus has allowed me to discover the advantages of using computers to help facilitate my projects and reports. Web page design and photo/slide scanning are such new discoveries that have greatly improved my understanding of the advantages of computer technology. A person can communicate with so many by creating a web page and using the internet to influence and inform others about topics that they are less knowledgeable about. My webfolio of images is meant to share with others the environment of Monterey Bay and the activities performed in my class SBSM 216/316.
