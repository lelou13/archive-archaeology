Old Memories, Tucson, Arizona. Old Memories Car Club members at Gates Pass near Tucson, Arizona. Campo Santo, Tumacacori, Arizona. The campo santo or cemetery compound of the mission of San Jose de Tumacacori, Arizona, incorporates an ossuary or mausoleum-like circular structure constructed in the early 19th century. Photo copyright © Ruben G. Mendoza, 1992. Catalog 2.046.

Pueblo Kiva. Interior of an indigenous ceremonial chamber, or Puebloan kiva, from the Pueblo of Nuestra Senora de los Angeles de Porciuncula de los Pecos, or Pecos Pueblo, New Mexico. Within such chambers katchina ceremonials were conducted. Note firebox in foreground. Photo copyright © Ruben G. Mendoza, 1991. Catalog 2.009.
