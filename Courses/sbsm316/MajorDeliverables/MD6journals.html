Major Deliverable 6
Lab Deliverable Participation/Online Journal
(Cut and Paste Journal Entries)

Objectives: To provide students with a venue in which to document the ongoing learning experience and questions related to class content covered through the course of the semester.  Each class meeting of SBSM 216/316 will entail the posting of your comments to the Online Journal located at: http://villa.monterey.edu/courses/sbsm316/journal.htm.  The posting of comments will occur during class time, and the content of such comments will reflect your learning experience for the particular day the comments were posted.  Because such comments will be used to judge your ongoing understanding of class projects, readings, and related materials, it is critical that you post your comments as fully and clearly as possible on an ongoing basis.
  • Full Name
  • Lab Deliverable name or entry title (e.g., Photo.net Comment 1)
  • Comments: Include a brief identification of the topic or subject being addressed.  Provide a summary of the learning experience had for the particular session during which the comments were posted.  Close your comments with a brief statement that serves to underscore what you saw as the most significant aspects of your learning experience for the particular day on which you posted your comments.
  • Take note of the specific questions or comments that the professor will post from time to time...be prepared to respond so that we can keep the dialogue or discussion moving forward.
