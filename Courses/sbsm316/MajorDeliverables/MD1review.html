Major Deliverable 1
Text Readings Review and Essay
(4 page summary)

Objective: To demonstrate your working knowledge and understanding of photographic processes and methods presented in readings and undertaken in class.  You will provide a technical description of your understanding and use of at least two photographic methods or techniques drawn from the areas of (a) documentary, (b) studio, (c) field, or (d) lab photography.

Content: In preparing your essay, you should identify the specific aspects of the type of photographic methods, materials, and equipment deemed most appropriate to the subjectmatter or photographic application undertaken (i.e., object or artifact photography requirements and or recommendations including backgrounds, lighting methods, film types, and the like).  The main objective here is to get a general sense of what you deem to be required and appropriate to the differing types of photography selected for review.  Your essay should make specific reference to readings and content from the required texts.

Photographic Methods: The two photographic approaches reviewed by way of your essay may concentrate on any of the following areas of technical imaging and photography:

  • Digital imaging
  • Object or macro photography
  • Flat copy or historic photo reproduction
  • Site photography
  • Architectural photography
  • Flash or reflected light photography
  • Studio photography
  • Photography for publication
  • Ethnographic or survey photography
  • Travel photography
  • Modelling or portfolio photography
  • Documentary photography
  • Micro photography
