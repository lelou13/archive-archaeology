Major Deliverable 7
End Term Webfolio/Portfolio
End Term Portfolio Includes All Term Deliverables
(hard copy & diskette):

Objective: To provide students with an opportunity to organize and provide a professional presentation of all project materials completed during the course of the semester.  The webfolio is essentially an Internet-based class and photo portfolio that serves to document the results and outcomes of the overall learning experience.

Content:  Though content will vary from one webfolio to the other, the primary content will consist of a digitized version of all photographs and written deliverables completed to the end of the term, as well as the primary theme-based photo essay (Major Deliverable 4) required by the end of the term.

Requirements: Webfolios or online portfolios will include web versions of all deliverables and photographs or digital images produced by you during the course of the semester.  The webfolio or online portfolio should include the following:

  • All web content contained within a single web folder titled by photographer last name and year (e.g. Mendoza2000)
  • Index page (i.e., main or "home" page within webfolio main folder)
  • Linked Table of Contents or Menu page (menu may be incorporated into Index page)
  • A brief Photographer Bio or Resume with photo (narrative or bulleted with photo optional)
  • Major Deliverables linked by way of menu (e.g. Major Deliverable 4 photo essay should be accessible by way of the Menu titled Major Deliverable 4)
  • Photo essays should be formatted so as to allow for efficient or smooth transitions from one image to the other (photos should include related caption information)
  • Related links page (optional)
Image Requirements: All images should be organized into a theme and captioned.  Captions should include the following information:

(a) title of photograph,
(b) description of content, event, or activity (as per theme),
(c) location,
(d) date produced,
(e) type of camera and film employed in production of image, and
(f) your name and copyright information.

*See your professor for the actual posting of the online portfolio.
