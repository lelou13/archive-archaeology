Ghana’s Decline
nAD 1076: Berber (Moroccan Almoravid) army attacks Ghana.
nReligious Reformers: Fundamentalist Islamic leaders behind invasion.
nConsequences of Invasion: Conflict and disorganization within Ghana.
nAD 1087: Ghana declines and loses control of empire to the Soninke.
nDisintegration: Ghana collapses into smaller states: Kangaba and Mali
