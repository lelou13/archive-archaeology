West African Empires: Ghana
nAncient States: Ghana, Mali and Songhai emerge in tandem with other states: Nok, Mossi, Kanem.  Succession from one to another via conquest & decline.
nGhana: Rises to state status at AD 300
nEconomic Basis: Trade in salt and gold; strategic position in trans-Saharan trade north to Morocco and Libya; West to Bornu region near Lake Chad & Nile Valley.
