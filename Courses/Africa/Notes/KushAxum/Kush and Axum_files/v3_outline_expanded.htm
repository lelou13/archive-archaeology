Group Work: Axum
Pair off with a partner with whom you are prepared to discuss Axumite civilization.
With 20 minutes for discussion, please review the instructions posed in the following frame of this slide show.
Finally, prepare questions about the Axumite vs Kushite civilizations that you are prepared to answer.
Each partner in the exercise should prepare to pose and answer at least one question.
Assignment: Summarize your responses to the two questions that you created in class.

Compare and Contrast
Describe the civilizations of Kush and Axum.
Compare and contrast the civilizations of Kush and Axum – How are they alike, and how do they differ?
Provide a reconstruction of life in the cities of the Kushites versus daily life in the cities of the Axumites.
Compare and contrast the religious and ritual systems of the Kushites versus those of the Axumites.
