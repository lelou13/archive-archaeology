Mansa Musa’s Legacy
•Mansa Musa (AD 1312-1332): Emperor of Mali expands empire through conquest.
•Consequences of Conquest: Doubling in size of empire and population by contrast with Ghana.
•Mansa Musa’s Empire: Expanded north to Mauritania and southern Algeria & south through Nigerian Hausa states.
•AD 1324: Musa undertakes epic hajj (pilgrimage) to Mecca.  Financed with 90 camel-loads of gold dust.  Depressed Egyptian gold standard for 10 yrs
