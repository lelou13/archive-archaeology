Musa’s Islamic Empire
•Mecca’s Influence: Mansa Musa returns from hajj with Muslim scholars, architects, and other skilled workers and craftsmen.
•as-Saheli: Architect and builder brought from Mecca by Mansa Musa.
•Timbuktu: as-Saheli builds University of Sankore at Timbuktu and helps to establish city as a major intellectual center of the Muslim world.
•Mali’s Decline: After AD 1400; Rise of Songhai from the vassal state of Gao.
