Sundiata Keita’s Legacy
•Sundiata Keita (AD 1230-1255): First king of Mali and subject of famous “Sundiata” epic.
•Agricultural Development: Mali established as rich farming area under Sundiata’s leadership.
•Farmer/Soldiers: Sundiata turns soldiers to agricultural development projects.
•Sundiata’s Control: Trans-Saharan trade and West African gold mining. Mandinka control.
•Niani: Established as capital and as key trade and financial center.
