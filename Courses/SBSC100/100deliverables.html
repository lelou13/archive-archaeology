SBSC 100A

Introduction to the Social and Behavioral Sciences

Deliverables

This learning experience does not require memory-based examinations or a final term paper project. As such, class readings, attendance, and the brief written assignments noted below will serve as the basis for your assessments.

  • Four brief typewritten research projects (of approximately 750 to 1000 words each) drawn from at least two of the subdisciplines of the social sciences
  • Two brief written descriptions of the content and assumptions presented in a social science article of your choice.
  • One end-term oral presentation or conference (Individual and or Group option)
  • One midterm and one end-term self assessment (Self Assessments)
  • Six major Deliverables

Note: All deliverables or assignments -- electronic and hardcopy byproducts of this core learning experience -- should be directed at your specific primary and secondary areas of inquiry and interest, and should attempt to address the specific skills areas required of your particular area of specialization. If you have yet to determine what specific area in the social sciences is of most interest to you, you may choose to address more than one primary theme in your brief essays.
