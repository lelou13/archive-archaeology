SBSC 100A

Introduction to the Social and Behavioral Sciences

Major Deliverable 2

Submission of two short research projects equivalent to on the one hand, a brief essay of between 750 and 1000 words; and on the other hand, either a second essay of similar length, or a project delivered in another medium such as internet web page, film, video, photo essay, sound recording or oral presentation. The precise form and content of any non-written project should be negotiated with the professor for whom the project is being prepared (i.e. Anthropology/Archaeology: Mendoza; Cultural Geography: Yong Lao; Social History: Shenk).

  • Note: For those that seek to enhance or further test their skills working with computers on the internet the second part of this project may include the development of a web page that features discussion, descriptions, images, and readings pertaining to topics and themes related to the social and behavioral sciences.

Date Due: Tuesday, October 22, 1996
