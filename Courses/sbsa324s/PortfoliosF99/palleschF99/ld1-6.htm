Author: Michael Palleschi.- Subject: LD4: Definitions.- 5 of 22Records Found.

Date: 9/23/99.- Time: 11:51:30 PM.- Entry Num: 239

(1)Datum - a fixed reference point for the entire excavation. (2)stratigraphy - a geological term that refers to strata (layers) of rock and soil and their placement relative to each other. (3)screening - sifting collected dirt from an archaeological site through mesh screening that ranges from 1/4, to 1/8 to 1/16 of an inch in the search of artifacts. (4)site grid - stakes and lines measured from the datum point that layout the site in a series of rectangles. (5) recording strate - taking samples of each layer of earth and rock.
