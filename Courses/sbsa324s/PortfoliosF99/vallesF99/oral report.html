Summary:
Why archaeology, well archaeology can help distinguish what the past was who the past was –and in addition to who and what the past was who and what the present consists of and their history.  It also plays a role in determining what and who the future will consist of.  There are many different things that can be found that are determining factors such as ceramics, pottery, and the soil and pollen.
 

Every Friday we would carpool to San Juan Bautista, and we would go to the front of the building right before you enter the mission and we would excavate.  I had a meter long unit for myself where I’d conduct excavations with a pick and later a larger one.  When we’d fill buckets of material we would then sift it because it was mainly soil.  To make things easier, but messier we could fill out units with water and that would enable us to dig easier and we could see what we had a little clearer.
We then took the specimen that we found and would catalog it on a 3x5 index card, where we’d describe everything about the specimen.
