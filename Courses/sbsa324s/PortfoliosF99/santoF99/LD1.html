In today's lecture the role maps play in archaeology was explained. They play a major role in the documentation of a site and it makes it easier for people in the future to find the site again. Also when lost, using both the compass and any map will help you figure out how to get out of the wilderness. Maps also help record the findings on the site and make it easier to catalogue different finds.
