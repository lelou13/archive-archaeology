Virtual Dig 1
      This was a very difficult project considering the lack of knowledge we have on the subject.  There are so many considerations. I guess each site would have different requirements so you just can't base other digs on one or two examples. The salaries seem to range from two-thirds to half of the overall budget. I think probably one of the most important aspects would be in deciding the areas to be excavated. That has to be done before anything else can be considered. This exercise showed me the basic order in planning an archaeological dig. The many things to take into account like food, accommodations, travel, equipment, salaries and autos were obvious essential. Some of the other considerations less obvious are specialists, insurance, airfare, and theodolite operators. I guess every detail must be taken into account.
