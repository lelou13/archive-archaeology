Major Deliverable 3: Lab and Field Journal Review


The main purpose of the Lab and Field Journal is to represent the work that has been completed up to date in class and in the field portion of the excavation and lab project. As such, this journal best reflects my continued active engagement and participation in the Lab, Class and Field activities and assignments. This journal also exemplifies my ability to document my experiences and findings, both coherently and precisely.

As the journal is reviewed, please notice that all fieldwork journal entries are labeled, "Excavation Day…" in the Table of Contents. Also notice the dates on the entries which represent the time and date those entries were made.  There are various entries that relate to class activities. Such as video reviews and class activities such as the mapping sampling strategy activity and the virtual dig. In addition to that, there are also the material assessment activities, which are lab deliverables. Other entries are Lab documentations, which are experiences and hands-on activities dealing with flintnapping in the Lithics lab. Other entries consist of documentations of lectures, such as the lecture on "Chichen" and Garbology.
