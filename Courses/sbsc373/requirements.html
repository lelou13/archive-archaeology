SBSC 273/373
Virtual Archaeology and GIS
Term Learning Requirements

This learning experience will encompass both Lab Deliverables (LD's) and Major Deliverables (MD's).  Lab Deliverables center on those in class and hands-on projects conducted during lab and class time.  Major Deliverables are those that center on term projects and research reports.  An example of the Lab Deliverable includes "LD1: Video Storyboard" in which you will review an archaeology video during class time and produce a "storyboard" of the primary learning themes and subthemes contained within the video.  An example of a Major Deliverable inlcudes "MD1: Project Prospectus" in which you will comment on the anticipated plan and outcomes of the multimedia project to be undertaken this semester.

Term Deliverables: The total number of Lab Deliverables will vary from term to term, and will depend on progress in meeting the goals of the learning experience.  This semester, we will target a total of approximately five Lab Deliverables, and four Major Deliverables.  Please bear in mind that the Lab Deliverables are intended to chart your progress in hands-on activities and participation and attendance.  As such, I will conduct in-class or field-based Lab Deliverables as these become necessary to assessing your participation and attendance.
