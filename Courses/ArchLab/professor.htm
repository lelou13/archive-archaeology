Professor’s Corner



This portion of the class website is devoted to posting links and commentaries about the Applied Archaeology Lab and our progress through the semester. I will in addition use this portion of the website to post general observations about the results of lab and field efforts, and from time to time I will link to group project reports and or deliverables and related exhibits for your review. The initial link is to my Speaker’s Bio for those who would like to know whose minding the podium. Related links and occasional postings related to your Professor’s research, the Institute’s programs, or past student projects will also serve as content for this portion of the site as well.
