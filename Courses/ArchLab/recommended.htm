Recommended Readings


The following readings have been posted here so as to provide you with a basic orientation to that literature that dominates the realm of Mission Archaeology and California Indian studies on the one hand, and archaeology lab and field methods on the other. Please bear in mind that while much of the following list is drawn from the field of archaeology, the reality is that Mission studies necessarily spans the fields of anthropology, historical archaeology, ethnohistory, architectural history, religious studies, linguistics, and a host of other areas of sociological and art historical analysis:


Cover photo of The Southwest. This volume includes a chapter on "Ethnicity" authored by Professor Mendoza and Dr. David Shaul.
