Announcements

Please frequent this portion of the website for announcements or news pertaining to class content or schedule changes and the like. Your professor will post those announcements and messages on this page that pertain to key activities that may also be announced by way of email messaging via archaeology_institute@csumb.edu. The most recent announcements or postings will appear as dated entries at the top of the page.

Welcome to SBS 224s/324s. The SBS 224s section is intended for Lower Division (Freshman / Sophomore) students wishing to address the Lab (METHODS) portion of the SCIENCE University Learning Requirement (ULR), and the SBS 324s section is intended for the Upper Division student wishing to expand their horizons or address the Service Learning ULR requirement and Major Learning Outcomes (MLOs) in the Social and Behavioral Sciences major.

In order to meet the requirements of the SBS 224s/ 324s course offering, all students are required to co-register for SBS 224L or 324L. Students enrolled in the SBS 224s section should co-register for the SBS 224L Archaeology Lab offering, and the SBS 324s students should co-register for the SBS 324L Archaeology Lab offering.

Online Service Learning Forms. please complete your required On-Line Placement Form in Planner Web to officially register the site where you are serving. Completion of this form is required in order to comply with CSUMB Risk Management policies. On this form, you are required to select your placement site from the list of pre-approved Community Partners that appear in the drop-down box. This form becomes the official record of your Service Learning placement for the semester.

Click here to access Planner Web: http://ar.csumb.edu/site/x4645.xml

If you have questions or experience any problems with this process please contact the Service Learning Institute at 582-3644.

Release and Waiver Forms. All students enrolled in SBS 224s/324s will be required to sign two forms. The first form pertains to a Liability Release and Waiver, and the second is a Multimedia Release and Waiver. The first form acknowledges that some of the lab projects may entail risks and or require that certain prescribed precautions be taken by any and all participants. The second form will permit your professor to document your class labs, field research, and lab experiences by way of photography or video and related multimedia. This latter form will permit your professor to make use of photographs or videography of you in future projects or publications.

If at any time during the semester you are concerned about projects that may entail necessary lab or field precautions, or require you to sort samples that you are concerned about handling despite being provided with safety gear, please do let your professor know so that he can assign you to other project activities.
