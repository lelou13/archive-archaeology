Note: This schedule is subject revision as per class interests and needs. Reader Response papers (RR) and Lab (LD) and Major Deliverable (MD) due dates are noted below. See Reader Response format at http://archaeology.csumb.edu/Guidelines/FormatGuides/sbs324sF03RR3Sample.pdf. Reader Response papers are not to exceed one typewritten single-spaced page per Unit or Chapter assignment.

Please consult the online version of this schedule on an ongoing basis in order to get updates on changes that may accrue. Because an electronic, or CD version, of the primary course text – Linking to the Past: A Brief Introduction to Archaeology – is available, please feel free to use this version of the text for readings. Most major concepts and definitions are generally highlighted or bolded in the text. See http://www.dushkin.com/online/contentsmain.mhtml for a useful guide to questions regarding the Annual Editions: Archaeology, 04/05 readings. Any and all readings pertaining to “Case Studies” noted below are to be selected from the Annual Editions: Archaeology, 04/05 text.

The electronic submission of all class deliverables completed to date will be required at both midterm and end term, and should be completed via electronic 3.5” diskette or CD copy. Otherwise, all deliverables must be submitted in hardcopy format for assessment by the specified deadlines noted below.

See http://archaeology.csumb.edu/Guidelines/AssessmentGuides/PortfolioAssessmentFormFnl.pdf for a sample of (final) Portfolio Assessment Criteria.
