Welcome to Archaeology From Map to Museum
Introduces the methods, principles, and practices of field archaeology by using exercises that stress strategy, interpretation, description, information management, archaeological technologies, and the role of scientific inquiry in the human sciences. Introduces the hands-on manufacture and use of stone tools and the interpretation of ancient and modern material cultures. Includes on-site field excavations and analysis of Hispanic colonial artifacts at the Old Mission of San Juan Bautista.
