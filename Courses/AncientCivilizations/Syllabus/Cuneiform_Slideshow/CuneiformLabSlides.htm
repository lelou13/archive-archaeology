Cuneiform Slide Show

This slide show includes images from the Cuneiform Lab of Spring 2005. The lab, which began as a pen-and-ink based effort to create Mesopotamian cuneiform texts was elaborated so as to include the use of a bamboo stylus and clay tablets. The thumbnail gallery below, and linked slide show, depicts students at work on their respective cuneiform tablets during the March 1, 2005 Cuneiform Lab exercise. All photos were produced by Professor Ruben G. Mendoza.


See the PDF version of the "Cuneiform Alphabet" for a translation of each and every script element pictured here. See the Mesopotamian numbers table for a translation of those year dates rendered in the tablets linked below.
