Axum: Urban Origins
uAxum: urban centres, indigenous writing, coinage (gold, silver, bronze), multi-storeyed masonry buildings, distinctive monumental architecture & sculpture, engineering skills, extensive trade, role in international politics of period, first state to accept Christianity, cultural continuity.
uRed Sea: Access to heart of Africa and Asia = rise of Axumite state
uIsolation: Ethiopia compared by Connah to that of a fortress (withstand seige, smite attackers)
