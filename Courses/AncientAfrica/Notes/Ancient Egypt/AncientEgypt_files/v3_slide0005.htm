Egyptian Unification
¨ Predynastic (4000-3100 BC): Complex patterns
emerge; trade, specialization, settlement…
¨ Archaic/Old Kingdom (3100-2160 BC): Upper &
Lower Egypt united politically; writing system;
architecture; administration; ideology…
¨ Menes (Narmer): U. conquers L. Egypt; theocratic
political systems dominate; capital at Memphis;
internal dissension @ 2900 BC despite powerful
kings; major construction programs pre-2700 BC.
¨ 2700 BC: nation-state, multi-tiered economy,
hierarchical bureaucracy, astronomy, geometry,
accounting, surgery, architecture…
