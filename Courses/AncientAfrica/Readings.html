Readings

Primary readings will be drawn from the following texts, as well as from supplementary readings and Internet sites assigned throughout the semester.  Recommended readers and readings are those found to be most useful to an overall understanding of Africa and its civilizations.

Required Texts

Connah, Graham
1987   African Civilizations: Precolonial Cities and States in Tropical Africa: An Archaeological Perspective.  New York: Cambridge University Press.

Ardouin, Claude Daniel (ed.)
1997    Museums & Archaeology in West Africa.  Washington, DC: Smithsonian Institution Press.

Optional Text

McEvedy, Colin
1995    Penguin Atlas of African History.  New Edition.  London: Penguin Books Ltd.

Assigned Readings

Schmidt, Peter R.
1995    Using Archaeology to Remake History in Africa.  In Making Alternative Histories: The Practice of Archaeology and History in Non-Western Settings.  Edited by Peter R. Schmidt and Thomas C. Patterson.  Pp.  119-147.  Santa Fe: School of American Research Press.
