Major Deliverable 1

Midterm Self Assessment


Description: Please prepare a Midterm Self Assessment of your learning experience in this class to date. The Self Assessment Statement should consist of a minimum of two typewritten pages of text that serve to document your learning, including how well you have kept up with text and or initiated independent research related to the Maya. Your Midterm Self Assessment is due: Tuesday, March 17, 1998.

Your self assessment should include some of the following considerations:


1. General statement about your learning experience to date.


2. Specific statement regarding your original objective in taking this course.


3. General statement regarding the extent to which you are meeting your original objective.


4. Specific statement about the research or theme that most interests you and the extent to which you are working toward developing a final project.


5. Specific statement regarding the extent to which the required readings assist you in developing an understanding of Maya culture and class themes.


6. General statement regarding your progress in conducting independent studies about the Maya beyond assigned class readings.


7. Specific statement regarding the extent to which you feel you are developing a broader understanding of Maya culture.


8. What you would like to see more of -- less of -- in class in so far as lecture, discussion, video and related course materials and presentation strategies.


9. What you feel you will need to do to improve your performance in the coming weeks and months.


10. Plans for how you intend to make this course relevant to your overall learning experience.
