Online Advising and Questions
You may e-mail your questions and requests for advising via the student e-mail accounts available to you. The "SBSC Classes" (SBSD 248/348) folder serves as a forum for depositing on-line assignments, and the SBSC Peer Counselors are available at Building 17 for your assistance.

Important Notice: When submitting deliverables and other assignments via the internet, please provide a clear and complete identification label for your submissions. For example: Joan Doe, who is registered in the lower division portion of the Spring 1998 Maya class, and who is submitting Major Deliverable 2, would title her e-mail submission and attachment as follows: sbsc248.doe.F98.md2 (upper or lower case letters are acceptable). Any assignment that does not contain the title format in question will not be accepted for evaluation.

All electronic submissions should be dropped into the "Class Folder" specific to the section within which you are enrolled: i.e., SBSC 248 submissions should be dropped in the SBSC 248 Classes Folder, whereas SBSC 348 students will submit their assignments in the SBSC 348 Classes Folder.
