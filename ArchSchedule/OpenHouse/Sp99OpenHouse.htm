Public Service Announcement

Museum Education Project Open House
Old Mission San Juan Bautista
Saturday, May 8th, 1999
10:00 a.m. to 2:00 p.m.

 

The CSU Monterey Bay Institute of Archaeology will be sponsoring its annual Museum Education Project Open House this Saturday, May 8th, 1999, from 10:00 a.m. to 2:00 p.m. The event, which is co-sponsored by Old Mission San Juan Bautista, CSUMB University Advancement and Center for Social and Behavioral Sciences, and the Gabilan Conservation Camp of Soledad, California, is free and open to the public. The Open House will feature guided tours of the archaeological and historical areas of the Old Mission, artifact displays, and complementary copies of an educational coloring book prepared by the students of the Museum Education Project of CSUMB. In addition, members of the Gabilan Conservation Camp will be on hand to discuss their efforts to enhance the Old Mission Museum and partake in the restoration of the Old Mission and its historic setting.

Dr. Ruben Mendoza, project archaeologist and CSUMB professor will be on hand to provide three tours of the Old Mission and its archaeological areas. Tours will take place at 10:00 a.m. (English), 11:30 a.m. (Spanish), and 1:00 p.m. (English), and students will be on hand to exhibit and discuss the discovery of recently recovered artifacts from Old Mission San Juan Bautista. At 12:30 p.m. and 1:30 p.m., students will be on hand to coordinate a children's activity and distribute a limited number of complementary educational coloring books prepared by CSUMB students during the course of this semester. The event will take place in the olive grove of the Old Mission Garden and courtyard. Access to the courtyard is by way of the Old Mission Gift Shop.

Schedule of Events:

10:00 a.m.: Guided Tour of the Old Mission and excavation areas by Dr. Ruben G. Mendoza, Archaeologist.

10:30 a.m. 2:00 p.m.: Ongoing student orientations to archaeology dig and artifact displays.

11:30 a.m.: Guided Tour -- in Spanish -- of the Old Mission and excavation areas (Guia En Espanol)

12:30 p.m.: Social Hour and Snack time in the courtyard.

Introduction of the new Old Mission San Juan Bautista: An Educational Coloring Book publication.

1:00 p.m.: Guided Tour of the Old Mission and excavation areas

1:30 p.m.: Children's "coloring book" activity & complementary coloring book.
