Project Schedule Spring 2001
Please note that the following project schedule is tentative, and is only intended to provide a general indication of the project lab and field schedule.  Though the schedule is subject to change as per illness, inclement weather, and other unforeseeable circumstances; those that are on the current semester's email list will be contact on the evening or morning just prior to the usual Friday field day in the event that any changes are made.  For those who are visitors to the Old Mission, this schedule should provide some sense of when the archaeology crew will be available for answering questions.  Field days normally extend from 8:30 a.m. to 4:00 p.m.

Note: Because the Spring 2000 field schedule is being conducted as an Independent Study for a handfull of CSUMB archaeology students wishing to continue with ongoing projects, all lab activities and processing will be conducted in the field this term.
