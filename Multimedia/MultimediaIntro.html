The CD Interface
The above interface design was created by the students of SBSC 373 (Virtual Archaeology &GIS) in the Fall of 1998.  The photos used in the above template were taken by Professor Mendoza, and the layout of the Old Mission quadrangle is based on archaeological findings from the excavations conducted by the CSUMB Institute of Archaeology under Professor Mendoza's direction.  The menu bar at the bottom of the screen includes images of Native Americans, Spanish Colonial Art, and an Image of Father Serra.
