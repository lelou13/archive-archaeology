Mission Ceramics

Mission Ceramics: A Virtual Type Collection is a new and innovative software solution that permits the user to review and study a “virtual type collection” of Mission era ceramic wares most commonly encountered in historical archaeology contexts. Drawing on recent advances in multimedia technology, this program incorporates video, audio, multimedia slide shows, virtual taxonomies, ceramics color charts, and an electronic flash card system including ceramic identifications, production dates, provenience, and descriptions of some 50 Mission era Hispanic, and later Mexican and early American ceramic types. In addition, Mission Ceramics affords teachers, students, and scholars with a ready source of ceramic identifications and color-corrected scale figures useful in archaeology and museum studies lab and field contexts.
