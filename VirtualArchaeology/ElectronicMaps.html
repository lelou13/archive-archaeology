San Juan Mission Map
Autocad Electronic Mapping

Using a Sokkia EDM (Electronic Distance Measurement) theodolite, Professor Mendoza of the Institute of Archaeology, and project volunteer Joe James,  have worked to develop an electronic mapping of all existing standing structures and subsurface archaeological remains that constitute the original mission era quandrange of Old Mission San Juan Bautista.  EDM maps prepared to date serve to document all building foundations excavated up through and including 1998.  Ongoing excavations continue to be documented on an "as needed" basis.  The screen capture on this page serves to document what said mapping looks like after having been imported from the Sokkia map software into Autocad 14.  Future versions of this mapping will include the original Northwest Convento wing wall and room block.  The ultimate aim of this mapping project is to reconstitute all data into a three dimensional (3D) mapping and virtual walkthrough of the mission at circa 1812.
