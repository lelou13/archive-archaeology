Mission Links
This page is devoted to providing updates and information regarding the Alta California Mission Research and Museum Education Projects at Old Mission San Juan Bautista.  In addition to providing a focal point for our postings regarding the Old Mission, and those projects being coordinated by Professor Mendoza and the CSU Monterey Bay Institute of Archaeology, this site will include information for educators and students who are interested in learning more about the Old Mission and community of San Juan Bautista, California.
