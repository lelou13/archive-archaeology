The Archaeology of Old Mission San Juan Bautista

This section of the site is intended to provide users of the "Old San Juan" CD-ROM with information and access on an ongoing basis.  As reports are presented or published in the future, copies will be posted here in this password-protected resources area of the Institute web site.  Those with the CD-ROM will be able to access this portion of the site via the password provided under the "Mission Archives" and "Archaeology Reports" section on the CD-ROM.  The first three reports published here serve to provide some background to the archaeological investigations at the Old Mission, particularly those that preceded the publication of the CD-ROM, and as such, provide a vantage point and rationale for why and when the investigations were conducted.
