Site Origins and Acknowledgements

This site was originally posted by Institute Director Ruben G. Mendoza in the Summer of 1995.  The site is maintained and updated on an ongoing basis by Professor Mendoza.  The web site underwent major revision in the Summer of 1997 with the installation of a web server and the revamping of the overall format of the site.  More recently, all Main Page layout and design was reformulated by Justin Evans.  The most recent revisions and additions -- including JavaScript and CGI capabilities and network connectivity -- were made possible by Christian Graves.  If you would like to make suggestions or submit comments, please feel free to sign our "Guestbook" located at the bottom of this site's "Main Page."  For Institute business or project-related questions or sponsorships, please feel free to contact the Institute Director at: archaeology_institute@csumb.edu
