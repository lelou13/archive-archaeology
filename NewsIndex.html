The Public Relations File
The following newspaper, magazine, and television news stories represents but a small cross-section of that press coverage generated as a result of the works of the students and faculty of the Institute of Archaeological Science, Technology, and Visualization of the California State University Monterey Bay. We would like to thank the following media sources for their generous coverage of the activities and projects of the Alta California Mission Research and Museum Education Projects of the ASTV.
